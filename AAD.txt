
Abstract :

      In this Application we will develop a City Explore Planner. So we can plan our Trips before we step out . Which for supose when we wanted to go on a trip to any part of the country , world this APP comes in handy. As here we add the Trip name we would go ex: South India then, we sellect and add the City location by search ex: Mumbai (Mumbai Railways)  and  then we can sellect and add the places near the location that are  displayed  ex:Hotels,restaurants.. etc. Here the App screen will display the saved trips .
